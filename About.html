Meet Josh

10-year old Joshua Agboola is a Programmer , Future Skills \ Activist, Knowledge Ambassador, Coding Coach, Author, and Keynote Speaker on technology topics. He is the CEO and Chief Programmer at Joshfortech a company he founded to provide web and app solutions to organizations and individuals. Joshua is considered a prodigy and tech polyglot. He started coding at the age of 6 and has built proficiency in the use of Scratch, Html5, CSS3, Javascript, Jquery, Bootstrap, Java, C++, C#, PHP, Laravel, and Unity. He also has proficiency in Lego Mindstorm, the programming of Arduino boards and the automation of devices using C++. He has built a few games, a healthcare app and a few websites to his credit. Joshua broke a record by becoming the youngest keynote speaker to present a talk at the ICTEL Expo of the Lagos Chamber of Commerce and Industry in July 2019. This was a first in the 131 years history of the Chamber and got him features in print and electronic media. He has also spoken at top conferences including Techpoint Inspired, Rise Networks and the Boys Mentoring Advocacy Network (BMAN)b alongside top CEOs and thought leaders. Joshua has been featured on First Bank’s First-class series and on TVC News and TVC spotlight documentary with trending reviews on social media. He has also been profiled in the MTN mPulse Planet magazine, Businessday, Vanguard, The Nation, AIT, NTA,LindaIkeji TV and Nairaland. He is a knowledge coach on Joshfortech, his new YouTube channel where he seeks to teach kids and adults technology and programming skills in an easy and fun to learn manner. Joshua sees possibilities in using technology to solve the world’s many pressing challenges and is passionate about getting young people to learn tech skills early.br
